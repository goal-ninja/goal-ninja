How Savings Work

Simple Savings Lock

Your savings lock and unlock automatically based on your performance. No settings to manage.

How it works
1

Miss a habit, add to savings

When you miss a habit check-in, the penalty amount goes into your savings vault for that goal.

2

Stay above your target rate

Each goal has a target completion rate (e.g. 80%). Hit or exceed it and your savings unlock.

3

Drop below? Savings lock again

If your completion rate falls below your target, savings lock back up until you get back on track.
